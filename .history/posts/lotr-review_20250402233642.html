Big changes are coming to my TikTok channel next month!

What's New

Starting April 1st, I'll be launching a weekly series called "60-Second Reviews" where I break down books in short, digestible videos. The twist? Each review will incorporate a different trending audio or format.

Schedule

New videos will drop every Tuesday and Friday at 5pm EST. The first month will focus on fantasy novels, with subsequent months exploring different genres based on audience polls.

Behind the Scenes

I'll also be sharing occasional "Bookstagram vs. Reality" clips showing my messy writing process and the chaotic reality behind curated book photos.
