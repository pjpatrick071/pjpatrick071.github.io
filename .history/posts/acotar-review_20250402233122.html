ACOTAR Review: Overhyped and Underwhelming

Posted on March 25, 2024 | Requested Review

Sarah J. Maas's "A Court of Thorns and Roses" has taken the book world by storm, but does it deserve the hype? In my opinion: not quite.

Problematic Romance

The central relationship between Feyre and Tamlin starts with concerning power dynamics that are never properly addressed. What's framed as passionate often crosses into controlling behavior that left me uncomfortable.

Worldbuilding Issues

While the faerie courts present an interesting premise, the rules of magic and politics feel inconsistent. Important elements seem to change based on what the plot requires in the moment.

Bright Spots

I will acknowledge Maas's ability to write action sequences, and some secondary characters (particularly Lucien) show glimmers of depth that made me wish they had more page time.

Final Rating: 2.5/5 - An okay fantasy romance but fails to live up to its reputation.
